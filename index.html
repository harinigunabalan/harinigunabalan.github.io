About Me

I am a Masters Student, specializing in Distributed Software Systems at the Technical University of Darmstadt, Germany. I was born and raised in India. I came to Germany for pursuing my Master Studies. My research interests include Cloud Computing, Distributed Systems, Big Data Analytics, Mobile Sensing and App development, Databases (Columnar & In-Memory), Computer Vision, Data Mining and Machine Learning.

I also work as a Graduate Student researcher at SAP Research, Products and innovation. As a productive compliment to my course at TU Darmstadt, I voluntarily interned at SAP SE, Center of Expertise (CoE), EMEA FI HCM/EPM.

Prior to my Masters I worked at Deloitte Consulting as a Business Technology Analyst for almost 2.5 years. I learnt ABAP Programming and delivered complex RICEF objects in MM FI and SD modules, in 5 major Client projects across the globe. I also mentored junior campus recruits during my tenure at Deloitte. I hold a Bachelor of Technology degree in Information Technology, from Madras Institute of Technology, Anna University.

Besides academics, I enjoy Travelling, Music, Art and History.
